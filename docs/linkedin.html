Corvallis Lions Club

Serving Corvallis, Oregon since 1925

The Corvallis Lions Club is dedicated to community service, leadership, and making a positive impact in Corvallis and beyond. For nearly a century, our members have supported local vision and health initiatives, youth programs, and humanitarian projects.

  • Provide free vision screenings and eyeglasses for those in need
  • Support local schools, youth, and community organizations
  • Organize service projects and fundraisers to benefit Corvallis
  • Foster leadership, friendship, and volunteerism

Join Us! We welcome new members who want to serve, lead, and connect.
